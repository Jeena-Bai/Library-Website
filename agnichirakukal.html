AGNICHIRAKUKAL

“Agnichirakukal” is the Malayalam translation of the renowned autobiography “Wings of Fire” written by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President of India. This inspiring book delves into the life and journey of Dr. Kalam, who rose from humble beginnings to become a prominent scientist and a visionary leader. The Malayalam translation, published in July 2018 as the 79th edition, brings this remarkable story to the Malayalam-speaking audience, allowing them to explore the extraordinary life of one of India’s most beloved figures.
