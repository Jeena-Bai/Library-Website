RAM C/O ANANDHI

The story revolves around Ram, a young Malayali, who arrives in Chennai to study at a film school, and the incidents that happen in his life with his new set of friends in the city. Incidentally, Mr. Dharmajan lived in Chennai for a couple of years and learned filmmaking, to gain experiences to write the book.
